Vulnerability Categories

The vulnerabilities found in the OWASP Juice Shop are categorized into several different classes. Most of them cover different risk or vulnerabiliy types from well-known lists or documents, such as OWASP Top 10.

OWASP top 10 security risks

  • [A1-2017] Injection
  • [A2-2017] Authentication
  • [A3-2017] Sensitive Data Exposure
  • [A4-2017] XML External Entities (XEE)
  • [A5-2017] Broken Access Control
  • [A6-2017] Security Misconfiguration
  • [A7-2017] Cross Site Scripting (XSS)
  • [A8-2017] Insecure Deserialization
  • [A9-2017] Vulnerable Components
  • [A10-2017] Insufficient Logging & Monitoring
